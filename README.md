# Sanctum 🏛️

**Sanctum** is a web application built for CSULB students to find and reserve campus study rooms — all in one place.

---

## What It Does

Finding a study room on campus usually means wandering around buildings or refreshing some outdated portal. Sanctum fixes that. Students can log in, browse an interactive campus map, pick a building and room, and book a time slot — without leaving the app.

Once a booking is made, students can view all their upcoming reservations and cancel them if plans change. There's also a profile page where you can manage your account info and upload a profile picture.

---

## Features

- **Authentication** — Sign up, log in, and sign out securely
- **Campus Map** — Browse buildings visually before selecting a room
- **Room Booking** — Pick a building → room → date → available time slot
- **Availability Check** — Only shows time slots that are actually open
- **My Bookings** — View and manage all upcoming reservations
- **Cancel Reservation** — Cancel a booking directly from your dashboard
- **Profile Management** — View and edit your account details, upload a profile photo
